About {{ turf.name }}

Welcome to SK Sports Turf, the premier destination for sports enthusiasts in the region. Established in 2018, we've been providing top-quality turf facilities for football, cricket, and other sports to players of all skill levels.

Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to offer the best playing experience with professionally maintained artificial turf that meets international standards. Whether you're organizing a competitive match, casual game with friends, or training session, SK Sports Turf provides the perfect environment.

  • Artificial grass with shock-absorbing technology
  • Modern changing rooms with shower facilities
  • Ample parking space for 25+ vehicles
  • Refreshment zone with healthy snacks and beverages
  • High-powered LED floodlights for night play

Our mission is to promote sports culture and provide accessible, high-quality facilities that inspire active lifestyles in our community.
